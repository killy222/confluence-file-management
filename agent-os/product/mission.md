# Product Mission

## Problem

Need to sync Confluence wiki pages (e.g. Phonix Sales, label "notebook") to Google NotebookLM and later manage these operations and their history in one place.

## Target Users

Internal team (e.g. sales) and operators who run or monitor the sync and related scripts.

## Solution

Extract from Confluence, push to NotebookLM with replace-if-exists; then a backend and dashboard to run scripts (separately or in chain), list exported files, and see when runs happened and whether they succeeded.
